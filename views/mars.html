Fun facts about Mars:

  • Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.
  • Its atmosphere is thin, dusty, and cold like a desert which supposedly used to be much wetter, warmer, and thicker.
  • This planet witnesses weather seasons and even has old volcanoes and canyons.
  • Mars is one of the most explored planets in our solar system.
