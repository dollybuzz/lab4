Fun facts about Earth:

  • So far, the Earth is the only planet we are aware of that is inhabited by living organisms.
  • Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
  • It is the fifth largest planet in our solar system.
  • Earth was the only planet not named after a Greek or Roman god/goddess. It is a Germanic word that means “the ground”.
